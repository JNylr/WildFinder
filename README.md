# GEAR - High Fantasy Extraction Looter

## Overview

**GEAR** is a high-fantasy extraction-based looter that combines the intense, mission-driven gameplay of *Helldivers* and *Arc Raiders* with the vibrant, fluid combat of *Wildstar* and *Wayfinder*. Team up with friends, dive into dangerous realms, defeat powerful enemies, and extract with legendary lootâ€”or lose it all.

## Core Gameplay Loop

### The Extraction Loop

Players embark on **runs** into vast, open fantasy maps filled with danger and opportunity. Each run follows this core loop:

1. **Deploy** - Drop into the map with your team (1-4 players)
2. **Explore** - Navigate the open world, discovering loot caches, hidden areas, and enemy encounters
3. **Combat** - Engage enemies and bosses using fluid, skill-based combat
4. **Collect** - Gather randomized loot from defeated enemies, treasure chests, and searchable locations
5. **Extract** - Successfully leave the map with your hard-earned gear, or...
6. **Die & Drop** - Fall in battle and lose all equipped loot from the current run

### High-Stakes Risk/Reward

Death has consequences. When you or your party is defeated, **all loot currently equipped or carried is dropped**. However, a potential **retrieval system** may allow players to drop back into the map for a high-risk rescue mission to recover their lost gear.

## Character Progression

### Specialized Roles

Players can specialize into distinct combat roles, each with unique abilities and playstyles:

- **Tank** - Frontline defenders who draw aggro and protect the team
- **DPS (Damage Dealer)** - High-damage specialists focused on eliminating threats quickly
- **Healer** - Support role that keeps the team alive through healing and buffs
- **Additional Roles** - *(To be expanded: crowd control, ranged specialists, hybrid classes)*

Each role will have unique skill trees, abilities, and equipment tailored to their playstyle.

### Gear Crafting & Upgrading

- **Craft gear** from materials and loot collected during runs
- **Upgrade equipment** to enhance stats, unlock new abilities, and improve effectiveness
- **Customize builds** to match your preferred playstyle and role
- Gear progression is essential for taking on tougher content and more dangerous bosses

### Base Building & Progression

Your **base** serves as your hub between runs and a key progression system:

- **Level up your base** to unlock access to more powerful crafting recipes
- **Upgrade facilities** to craft higher-tier weapons, armor, and consumables
- **Unlock new tiers** of equipment needed to challenge the most dangerous bosses
- Base progression gates access to end-game content, creating a meaningful long-term goal

## Combat System

### Fluid, Skill-Based Combat

Combat is designed to feel **responsive, dynamic, and satisfying**:

- **Balanced pacing** - Not too fast to be chaotic, not too slow to feel sluggish
- **Smooth animations** - Seamless transitions between attacks, dodges, and abilities
- **Skill expression** - Rewarding player timing, positioning, and decision-making
- **Team synergy** - Roles complement each other, encouraging coordinated play

The combat system draws inspiration from action-oriented MMOs, emphasizing player skill and teamwork over simple stat checks.

## World & Content

### Dynamic Open Maps

- **Large, explorable environments** filled with diverse biomes and landmarks
- **Randomized loot spawns** ensure every run feels different
- **Scalable difficulty** - Harder zones contain tougher enemies and better rewards
- **Boss encounters** - Epic battles against powerful foes that drop rare, high-tier loot

### Loot System

- **Randomized drops** - Every run offers different loot opportunities
- **Tiered rarity** - Common to Legendary gear with increasing power and unique effects
- **Searchable containers** - Chests, crates, and hidden caches scattered throughout the world
- **Boss-specific loot** - Unique items and materials from defeating challenging bosses

## Multiplayer
